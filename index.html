Cutting-edge L&D specialist for the AI era.

I'm Kai Saks, an L&D specialist and AI Enablement expert based in Austin, TX. My passion is building the programs that make learning real. For over a decade I've worked inside high-growth fitness and health brands, leading training at scale across 50+ countries. The last several years have centered on helping organizations actually become AI-ready, designing the frameworks, curricula, and change management programs that bridge the gap between AI curiosity and genuine adoption.

I bring a rare combination: the structure of a project manager, the user-first lens of a product thinker, and the forward-looking fluency of someone who's been in the AI space before most teams knew they needed it.
